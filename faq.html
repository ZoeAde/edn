ēdn FAQ

How much energy does this use?

Pretty much nothing at all. If you leave 1 light on, everyday then you are using 4 times as much energy as the WallGarden. That’s because we are using the most energy efficient ways to grow.

Do I have to buy your plants?

Nope. While we’ve done a really good job of sourcing high quality non-GMO seeds we also understand if you want to plant your own seeds using our blank seed kit.

How do I hang it?

We have a simple 1 x 3 inch mounting bracket that is placed on your wall. The unit is then hung on that bracket. It’s rather easy.

How big is it?

The WallGarden is 30 x 32 x 8 inches and weighs about 15 lbs when empty, with water it’s about 35 lbs.

What kind of sound / light does it give off?

All the lights are LEDs so they are low energy and they emit a yellow/white light rather than blue/violet light which we’ve found to be harsh. The bubbling is audible and sounds like a small fish tank.

Do I need to use the mobile app?

To configure it, yes. Beyond that the app is optional but strongly encouraged.

What’s it made out of?

We use wood and aluminum for the handcrafted construction. Inside each shelf is a plastic linear that is removable and easy to clean.

Is there any dirt?

Nope. Rather than using dirt we put each seed into a composted organic material that is plant based. The entire pod is immersed in oxygen rich water for a wonderful growing environment.

How often do I have to water it?

The system takes care of the water and will notify you when water needs to be added to the reservoir. It’s possible to go 10 days or more without adding water to the unit.
